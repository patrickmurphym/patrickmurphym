<h2 align="left">Hi there! ✨ My name is Patrick J. Murphy Munoz </h2>

I'm an **engineer-turned-analyst** in **private real estate lending**, focused on **turning portfolio complexity into clarity**. Over the past few years, I've worked in asset management and portfolio analysis, helping teams **see what's coming, act early, and make better decisions faster**.

The core of my work is **building analytic frameworks** that give lenders **visibility into their portfolios** while **reducing operational friction**. 

- I've built a **watchlist system** that **flags upcoming maturities** and surfaces **extension opportunities** before they become urgent. This is the kind of **early-stage monitoring** that shifts **reactive crisis management to proactive risk mitigation**.
- From there, I've moved into **financial modeling** and **automated reporting systems** using **Python and Visual Basic for Excel**. These tools **compress decision cycles** and **free teams from manual processing**, so they can focus on **strategy and risk management** rather than spreadsheet maintenance.

I hold a **Master's in Financial Engineering** from **NYU** and a **Bachelor's in Industrial Engineering** from **Universidad Adolfo Ibanez**. The combination shapes how I think: **engineering rigor** applied to the messiness of capital markets, with **operational efficiency** baked into every framework I build.
